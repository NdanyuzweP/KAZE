Our Story

The Meaning of KAZE

In Kinyarwanda, "KAZE" means welcome -. Just like the wind, our coffee shop aims to bring a fresh perspective to coffee culture. Founded in 2022, we've been dedicated to sourcing the finest beans directly from our land.

Our Philosophy

We believe in creating more than just a cup of coffee - we craft experiences. Every bean is carefully selected, expertly roasted, and prepared with precision to unlock its unique character and flavor profile.
